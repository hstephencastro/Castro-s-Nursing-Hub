NURS-0243 · ADN Program

Complex Nursing Concepts

Medical-surgical nursing for complex patients — CJMM-aligned, NGN-ready

Course Overview

What You'll Learn

Complex Nursing Concepts prepares you to care for medically complex adult patients using the NCSBN Clinical Judgment Measurement Model (CJMM). All content is aligned with Next Generation NCLEX (NGN) question formats — including SATA, matrix, bow-tie, and highlight questions.

💡 Professor Castro's CJMM Tip
Always work through the 6 CJMM steps in order: Recognize cues → Analyze cues → Prioritize hypotheses → Generate solutions → Take actions → Evaluate outcomes. When you're stuck on an NGN question, slow down and ask: "What step am I being asked about?"
