NURS-0108 · ADN Program

Pharmacology III

Advanced drug therapy — cardiovascular, renal, GI, immunity & beyond

Course Overview

What You'll Learn

Pharmacology III builds on your foundation to tackle advanced and complex drug therapy. You'll apply the CJMM framework to real clinical scenarios, learning to prioritize drug safety, recognize interactions, and make sound clinical judgments at the bedside.

💡 Professor Castro's Study Tip
For every drug you study, run through the 3×3 Framework: What are the Top 3 nursing concerns? What are the Top 3 nursing implications? If you can answer those two questions for every drug class, you're thinking like a safe nurse.
