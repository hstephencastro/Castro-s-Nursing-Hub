NUPN-0200 · PN Program

Clinical Judgment

Developing the thinking skills that define great nurses — using the CJMM

Course Overview

What You'll Learn

Clinical Judgment prepares PN students to think critically, recognize clinical deterioration, and respond with safe, prioritized actions. Using the NCSBN Clinical Judgment Measurement Model (CJMM) as our framework, you'll develop the systematic thinking patterns that safe nurses use every single shift.

💡 The Most Important Thing I Can Tell You
Clinical judgment isn't something you either have or you don't — it's a skill you build with practice. Every case study you work through, every time you stop and ask "what should I do FIRST and WHY," you're training your brain to think like a nurse. The repetition is the point.

THE 6 CJMM STEPS
1️⃣ Recognize cues — what do I notice?
2️⃣ Analyze cues — what does it mean?
3️⃣ Prioritize — what's most urgent?
4️⃣ Generate solutions — what could I do?
5️⃣ Take actions — what do I do now?
6️⃣ Evaluate — did it work?
